Bali Holiday Travel Package - 5 Days / 4 Nights

Location: Bali, Indonesia

Duration: 5D / 4N

Price: ₹45,000 per person

Overview

Enjoy a memorable 5-day holiday in Bali with beautiful beaches, temples, waterfalls and adventurous activities. This all-inclusive package covers hotels, sightseeing, meals, and airport transfers.

Day-wise Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival at Bali – Hotel Check-in

Warm welcome & transfer to hotel. Evening free for leisure.

Day 2: Full Day Kintamani Volcano Tour

Check-out & airport drop.
